What is HuaDie?

A project to help people face death and recall the past.

Why is it called HuaDie?

HuaDie means turning into a butterfly. Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai are beautiful folk love stories in China. They can't be together for various reasons. But when they died, they became butterflies together. So I want to use butterfly to refer to these beautiful things or memories.
